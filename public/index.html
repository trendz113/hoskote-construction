Hoskote guidance

Building approvals in Hoskote — what you need to know

Hoskote comes under Gram Panchayat jurisdiction — not BBMP. This changes what approvals you need and who you apply to.

🏛️

Gram Panchayat Building Approval

  • Required before starting any construction in Hoskote
  • Apply at local Gram Panchayat office with site plan
  • Fees: approx ₹20,000–₹60,000 depending on plot size
  • Timeline: 30–60 days typically
  • If layout is BMRDA-approved, process is smoother
📋

Khata & Property Registration

  • E-Khata / E-Swathu required for Gram Panchayat areas
  • Check if plot has A-Khata or B-Khata status
  • DC conversion needed if agricultural land
  • Registration stamp duty ~5.6% of property value
  • Can affect home loan eligibility — check first
⚡

Water, Electricity & Borewell

  • BESCOM connection: ₹15,000–₹25,000 approx
  • Temporary construction connection available
  • Borewell permission from local authority
  • BWSSB connection if in pipeline area
  • Overhead tank planning from start saves cost later

We know the Hoskote Panchayat process well. We can guide you on the exact documents needed, connect you with the right contacts, and help you avoid common delays — at no extra charge.

What if you take advance money and don't complete the project?
We never ask for large advance payments. Every payment is tied to a stage — you pay only after you see the completed work and approve it. This is written in your agreement. We have no reason to disappear: our business and reputation are in Hoskote.
How much advance do you take at the start?
We take a small mobilisation advance (typically 10-15%) to start material procurement and labour. After that, every payment follows a milestone: foundation complete, slab complete, walls complete, finishing complete. You'll see photos before each payment request.
Can I visit the site while construction is going on?
Yes, absolutely. You can visit whenever you want — no need to inform us in advance. The site is always open to you. And even when you can't visit, you'll see daily photos on WhatsApp so you're never in the dark.
What if I'm not happy with the quality at any stage?
You approve each stage before the next one starts. If you're not satisfied, we redo it. You never pay for a stage you haven't approved. This is the whole point of stage-wise payment — your approval gates every rupee we receive.
Do you handle Gram Panchayat and BMRDA approvals?
Hoskote comes under Gram Panchayat jurisdiction for building plan approval — not BBMP. If your layout is BMRDA-approved, that makes the process smoother. We can guide you on exactly what documents the local Panchayat office needs, connect you with the right people, and help you avoid common delays. Approval fees are separate from construction costs but we'll walk you through the full process.
Can I get a home loan for construction in Hoskote?
Yes. We can help connect you with banks familiar with Hoskote plots — SBI, HDFC, Canara Bank, Karnataka Bank. Construction loans work differently from flat purchase loans, and we can walk you through what banks need from a construction project.
How much total money do I need beyond just the construction cost?
Good question — many families budget only for construction and get surprised. Besides construction cost, plan for: plot registration stamp duty (~5.6%), Gram Panchayat plan approval (₹20,000–₹60,000), BESCOM electricity connection (₹15,000–₹25,000), compound wall and gate (₹1–2 lakh), and borewell if needed.
